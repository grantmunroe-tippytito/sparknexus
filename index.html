About Me

I'm a journeyman motorcycle technician with a passion for engines, mechanics, and the open road. My journey started in Toronto, where I grew up learning the ins and outs of what makes things tick.

My Educational Path

My technical education took me across Canada:

  • Central Tech High School - Toronto, where it all began
  • Canadore College - Eco-tourism Certificate
  • Grande Prairie Regional College - Harley-Davidson factory training and Journeyman Certification
  • Red Deer Polytechnic - Completing Network Administration Diploma

Each institution helped shape me into the technician I am today, and I'm grateful for the diverse experiences and knowledge I gained along the way.

Meet Tippy

No introduction would be complete without mentioning my four-legged companion, Tippy. Whether I'm working in the garage or taking a break, Tippy is always nearby, bringing joy and companionship to every day.

There's something special about the bond between a technician and their dog - Tippy knows when it's time to work and when it's time to play. A true shop dog at heart!

My Passion

Working on motorcycles isn't just a job - it's a calling. There's nothing quite like diagnosing a problem, getting your hands dirty, and bringing a machine back to life. Every bike has its own personality, and every repair is a new puzzle to solve.
